 Had I left the room a minute, when his man told me that Young Innocence (so he called you, and you may call him Old Guilt all the days of your life) had set his heart upon her, and she was giddy and liked him, but his master was resolved that no harm should come of itmore for your sake than for hersand that that was their business here?
